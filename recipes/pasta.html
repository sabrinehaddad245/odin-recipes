Pasta

Description

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add pasta to the boiling water and cook according to package instructions.
  3. In a separate pan, sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato sauce to the pan and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Drain the cooked pasta and add it to the sauce. Mix well.
  6. Serve hot, topped with grated cheese, salt, and pepper to taste.
