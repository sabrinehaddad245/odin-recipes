Couscous

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 cup couscous
  • 1 1/4 cups water or broth
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or butter
  • Salt to taste
  • Optional: chopped vegetables, herbs, or spices for flavor

Steps

  1. Bring water or broth to a boil in a medium saucepan.
  2. Add salt and olive oil or butter to the boiling liquid.
  3. Remove the saucepan from heat and stir in the couscous.
  4. Cover the saucepan and let it sit for 5 minutes.
  5. Fluff the couscous with a fork and add any optional ingredients for flavor.
  6. Serve warm as a side dish or base for other dishes.
